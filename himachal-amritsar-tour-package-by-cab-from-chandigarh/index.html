About Itinerary

Itinerary given here is the most selling and recommended one; we have selected the most common choices and plans of our recent visitors. This itinerary can be modified, if you want to customize it according to your plans.

Day - 1
Chandigarh to Shimla (Day journey by cab)

Arrive at Chandigarh Airport / Railway Station by morning time. Meet our driver and start your journey towards Shimla. Day journey by cab from Chandigarh to Shimla. Reach in Shimla by evening time. On arrival check-in to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 2
Full day Kufri & Shimla local sightseeing by cab

Today morning leave the hotel and start traveling towards Kufri. Kufri is one of the very popular destinations of Shimla and is located at a distance of approximately 20 KM from Shimla about a one hour journey by road. Visit Himalayan Nature Park, Kufri Fun World, Amusement Park, Kufri Zoo, and Mahasu Peak. Also, enjoy horse riding to the Mahasu Peak. Afternoon time returns back to Shimla and visits Shimla local places like- The Famous Ridge Road, The Mall Road, Gaiety Heritage Cultural Complex, Indian Institute of Advanced Study, and The famous Lakkar Bazar. Evening time visit Kali Bari temple and after darshan returns back to the hotel.Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 3
Shimla to Manali (Day journey by cab)

Today Morning check-out from a hotel and drive towards Manali. Day journey by cab from Shimla to Manali. On the way Kullu valley sightseeing like- Kullu River Rafting point, Kullu Vaishno Devi temple, Hanogi Mata Temple, Sunder Nagar lake, and Pandoh Dam. Also visit Naggar Castle. Naggar is one the most visited destination in Kullu District. It is an ancient heritage village (popular for the Naggar Castle). Naggar Castle is a unique example of the Western and Himalayan style of architecture. The entire campus is made up of Wood and stones. It also provides a 360° view of the surrounding areas. Also, visit Tripura Sundari Temple, Roerich art gallery & Museum, Uruswati Himalayan Folk Art Museum, and Gauri Shankar Temple. Reach in Manali by evening time. On arrival Check-in to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 4
Full day Solang Valley & Manali local sightseeing by cab

Today morning leave the hotel and start traveling towards Solang Valley. Solang Valley is the most visited destination in the Manali region, located at the distance of Approximately 14 KM from the main town). This place serves with a wide range variety of activities and adventure sports. Here you can enjoy paragliding, Zorbing, skiing, at a very nominal cost. There are overhead cable car rides available as well and you can also enjoy horse riding too. Also visit Anjani Mahadev Temple there. After that return back to Manali and visit Manali local places. On the way, Vashisht Temple & village, Take a bath in hot water spring there. After that visit The Famous Hidimba Devi Temple, Van Vihar, Club House, Manu Temple, and Tibetan Buddhist monastery. Evening time visit the famous Mall Road and after doing some shopping returns back to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 5
Manali to Dharamshala (Day journey by cab)

Today Morning check-out from a hotel and drive towards Dharamshala. Day journey by cab from Manali to Dharamshala. On the way Palampur Valley Sightseeing. Visit- Palampur Tea garden, Baijnath Temple, and Chamunda Devi temple. Reach in Dharamshala by evening time. On arrival Check-in to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 6
Half Day Dharamshala local sightseeing & Dharamshala to Dalhousie by cab

Today morning check-out from the hotel and start traveling towards Dharamshala local places. Visit- Dharamshala Cricket Stadium, St. John in the Wilderness Church, War Memorial, Gyuto Monastery, Dal lake, and Nadi, Tea gardens, Bhagsu Nath waterfalls, Tsuglagkhang, Mcleodganj, Namgyal Monastery, Mcleodganj, Bhagsu Nath temple, Tibet Museum, Mcleodganj, Kangra Art Museum, Dalai Lama Temple Complex, and Aghanjar Mahadev Temple. After that drive towards Dalhousie. Reach in Dalhousie by evening time. Drive towards the hotel, Check-in to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel

Day - 7
Full day Khajjiar & Dalhousie local sightseeing by cab

Today morning leave the hotel and start traveling towards Khajjiar. Khajjiar is also known as Mini Switzerland. On arrival visit- Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary, Khajjiar lake, Golden Devi temple, Khajji Nag temple, and Khajjiar 9 hole Golf Course. Spend some time near the Khajjiar lake, take photographs, and enjoy walking in the thick pine forest. Also participate in some adventure activities like- Paragliding, Zorbing, and Horse riding. After that drive towards Dalhousie and visit Dalhousie local places. Visit- Panch Pulla, Satdhara waterfalls, Subash Baoli, Bakrota Hills view Point, and St. John Church. Evening time visit Dalhousie market and after doing some shopping returns back to the hotel. Overnight stay in a hotel.

Day - 8
Dalhousie to Amritsar & Amritsar local sightseeing by cab

Today Morning check-out from a hotel and drive towards Amritsar. Reach in Amritsar by afternoon time and visit Amritsar local places like – Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum, Partition Museum, Silver temple (Lakshmi Narayan temple), Gobindgarh Fort, Mata Lal Devi temple, and Khalsa College. Evening time visit Wagah Border and witness the Beating Retreat Ceremony after that drive towards hotel, Check-in to the hotel. Overnight stay at a hotel.

Day - 9
Amritsar to Chandigarh drop by cab

Today Morning check-out from a hotel and drive towards Chandigarh. Journey by cab from Amritsar to Chandigarh. Reach in Chandigarh by afternoon time and drop at Chandigarh Railway Station /Airport.
